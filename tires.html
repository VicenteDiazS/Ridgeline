Stock Rim Fitment Notes

Size Diameter Width Stock Rim Guidance
245/60R18 29.6 in 9.6 in OEM size. Best baseline for no-rub fitment.
255/60R18 30.0 in 10.0 in Forum-guided cautious upsizing. Still verify actual tire shape.
265/60R18 30.5 in 10.4 in Common forum maximum, but some all-terrain tires rub at full lock or reverse.
245/65R18 30.5 in 9.6 in Narrow but taller. Treat as measurement-required until checked on your truck.
255/65R18 31.1 in 10.0 in Likely clearance-sensitive. Use only after measuring and test fitting.

Forum guidance is not a guarantee. Tire brand, side lug shape, alignment, suspension height, tread type, wheel offset, and spacers can change whether a size rubs.
