Charging And Connectivity

  • All 2019 Ridgelines have 12-volt outlets in both the center stack and center console.
  • RT, Sport, and RTL trims use a 1.0-amp USB Audio Interface in the center stack.
  • The center-console USB on RT, Sport, and RTL became a 2.5-amp charging-only outlet for 2019.
  • RTL-T and above add a 1.5-amp USB Smartphone/Audio Interface in the center stack.
