This page is set up like a cargo quick-reference sheet. The bed dimensions below are the ones you will care about when checking if sheet goods, coolers, recovery gear, or a spare tire setup will fit without grabbing a tape measure first.

Bed Dimension Diagram

Key bed numbers to remember: 64 inches with the tailgate up, 83 inches with it down, and enough width to lay 4-foot material flat.

Useful Cargo Notes

  • The lockable trunk can fit large items such as an 82-quart cooler.
  • The drain plug lets you use the trunk as a cooler or rinse out wet gear.
  • The compact spare rides in the front area of the trunk on a sliding tray.
  • The dual-action tailgate swings open for easier trunk access in tight spaces.
