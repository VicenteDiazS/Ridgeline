    Model Notes

    The model is intentionally service-oriented: it emphasizes part relationships, visual orientation, and the recorded timing belt work instead of exact casting geometry. Component labels are anchored to the modeled part centers, timing-side locations follow the supplied service-belt reference, and major groups were cross-checked against Honda engine category diagrams. Exact casting texture, bracket shape, harness clipping, and hose routing remain stylized until a licensed J35Y6 or J-series scan is added.
