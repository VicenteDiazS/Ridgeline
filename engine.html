Model Notes

The model is intentionally service-oriented: it emphasizes part relationships, visual orientation, and the recorded timing belt work instead of exact casting geometry. Component labels are anchored to the modeled part centers and timing-side locations follow the supplied service-belt reference. If you later get a licensed J35Y6 or J-series scan, this page can be upgraded to use that asset while keeping the same hotspots and service data.
